Info

This recognizer may raise an error that it is still in the paused state. I do not know why.

It will create a JACK sink under the name "DEVICENAME_in". Recognized words will be posted to "/devices/NAME/final", as (phrase, confidence) pairs, in addition to being shown in the device's management page.

Keywords

Keywords the voice recognizer will look for. One per line. multi-word keyphrases are allowed. Requires "gstreamer1.0-pocketsphinx" or equivalent installed.

Thresholds may be supplied in pocketsphinx /1e-25/ format at the end of a line
